# MVP Concept - EduTrack Academic Management App

## Problem Statement
Students struggle to stay organized across multiple subjects, often missing deadlines and lacking visibility into their academic performance. Teachers spend excessive time manually tracking attendance and grades without a centralized system. There is no affordable, unified tool that connects assignment management, grade tracking, and AI-powered study guidance in one place.

## Target Users
- Students who need to organize assignments and monitor their academic progress
- Teachers who manage multiple classes and need to track student performance efficiently

## Core Features
- Assignment tracking with due date reminders and priority labels
- Grade calculator and GPA estimator with visual progress charts
- Attendance tracking for teachers with per-class session records
- AI-powered personalized study plan based on individual grade performance
- Real-time teacher dashboard to monitor class progress and flag at-risk students

## Constraints
- No payment integration in MVP — free to use
- Maximum of 500 students and 50 teachers per instance
- AI study plan requires at least 3 graded assignments to generate recommendations
- Mobile-responsive web app only — no native iOS or Android app in MVP
- Data stored for a maximum of 2 academic years per account
