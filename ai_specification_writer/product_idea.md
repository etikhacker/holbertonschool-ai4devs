# Product Idea

## Vision
Empower students and educators to track academic progress, manage assignments, and receive AI-powered study recommendations in one unified platform.

## Users
- **Students**: want to organize assignments, monitor their grades, and get personalized study plans to improve academic performance.
- **Teachers**: want to distribute assignments efficiently, track student progress, and identify students who need additional support.

## Key Features
- Assignment tracking with due date reminders
- Grade calculator and GPA estimator
- AI-powered personalized study plan recommendations
- Real-time progress dashboard for teachers and students
- Absence and attendance tracking
- Multi-class support for teachers managing multiple groups
