## Problem Statement
Students struggle to organize their study time effectively, especially when preparing for multiple exams or deadlines at once. An AI-powered study plan generator can analyze the student's subjects, available time, and priority levels to automatically create a personalized, day-by-day study schedule.

## MVP Features
- Input subjects, exam dates, and available daily study hours
- AI generates a personalized day-by-day study schedule
- User can mark topics as completed to track progress
- AI suggests study tips and resources for each subject
- User can regenerate the plan with updated inputs
